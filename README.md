# Macy's Web Analytics & E-Commerce Insights | Google Analytics · Excel · Data Visualization

## Project Overview
End-to-end Google Analytics analysis of Macy's e-commerce platform covering October 2019 to October 2020 — tracking KPIs across conversion rate, bounce rate, average order value, revenue, and user behavior to deliver actionable marketing recommendations.

## Business Problem
Macy's needed to understand why revenue and conversion rates were declining year-over-year and identify where to focus marketing spend to maximize customer acquisition and retention.

## Key Findings
- Overall conversion rate declined 10.51% YoY despite higher page depth and engagement
- Paid search delivered the highest conversion rate across all marketing channels
- Best conversion hour: 4 PM — optimal time for promotional campaigns
- Average Order Value (AOV) increased despite revenue decline — customers spending more per visit but fewer visits overall
- 47% of users from USA — India and Canada ranked 2nd and 3rd
- Age group 25-35 drives the majority of purchases
- Men's T-shirt and Home Page saw 40% increase in landing page conversions
- Goal conversion rate improved to 14.53% in 2020 vs 2019
- COVID-19 pandemic identified as primary driver of 2020 revenue decline

## Business Recommendations
1. Increase paid search investment — highest conversion channel across all traffic sources
2. Schedule promotions at 4 PM — peak purchase hour identified from hourly analysis
3. Improve mobile UX and website design to reduce bounce rate and increase page depth conversion
4. Launch loyalty/credit point program to retain returning users whose numbers are declining
5. Focus marketing spend on 25-35 age group — primary revenue demographic
6. Increase targeted campaigns for Men's T-shirts — highest converting landing page
